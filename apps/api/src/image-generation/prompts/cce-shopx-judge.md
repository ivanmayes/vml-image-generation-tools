## OBJECTIVE REFERENCE SPECIFICATIONS (Ground Truth)

### Brand Colors & Identity

- Coca-Cola red background: #FE001A (vibrant red, NOT orange, NOT maroon, NOT dark red)
- Logo: White Spencerian script on solid red rectangle background
- Logo aspect ratio: Fixed 4:3 (both horizontal and vertical orientations)
- Logo clear space (horizontal): 1/4x on each side (x = longer edge of logo)
- Logo clear space (vertical): 1/5x on each side (x = longer edge of logo)
- The Coca-Cola logo must NEVER be distorted, rotated, recolored, or partially obscured

### Typography Rules

- Headline: Maximum 12 characters, spread over 2-3 lines
- Headline minimum font size: 18pt (no smaller on any material)
- Legal line minimum font size: 5pt for print (7pt recommended)
- Legal line: 3-6 lines, positioned at 1/40a margin from edges (a = shorter edge of image)
- Text must adapt to textbox by adjusting font size for maximum fill
- Textbox must NOT exceed the non-infringement area (clear space)

### Product Permutation Rules

- Coca-Cola (leading brand) product must be at the forefront, center position
- Other participating brand products arranged symmetrically behind/beside
- ALL product logos must be clearly visible and recognizable
- Overlap between products must NOT exceed 10% of bottle width
- Product proportions must reflect actual capacity size differences
- Maximum 5 products in a single composition
- Minimum 1 product (can be 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5)
- Two-brand collaboration: leading brand forefront, partner brand to the right/rear
- Three+ brand collaboration: leading brand ALWAYS at forefront

### Layout Zone System (ShNRED Principle)

Each ad material falls into one of three zones with specific prioritization:

**Transition Zone** (Store entrance/windows):

- Priority order: ENGAGE > SEE > CLOSE
- "Engage" section (lifestyle imagery) should be prioritized/largest
- Materials: Window sticker (1:1), Entrance poster (1:2), A-board (1:2), Counter wrap (3:1)

**Impulse Zone** (Floor displays/point-of-purchase):

- Priority order: SEE > ENGAGE > CLOSE
- "See" section (brand/product) should be prioritized/placed higher
- Materials: Floor display (2:1), Cut case (2:1 or 1:2), Tent card (1:2), Table sticker (2:1), Meal combo poster (2:1)

**Destination Zone** (Shelves/coolers/digital menus):

- Priority order: CLOSE > SEE > ENGAGE
- "Close" section (headline/CTA) should be prioritized/centered
- Materials: Cooler (2:1), Main shelf price tag (3:1), Wobbler (2:1), Digital menu (2:1)

### Layout Section Definitions

- **SEE section**: Contains Coca-Cola logo (red rectangle) + product lineup images. Fixed proportion relative to total area.
- **ENGAGE section**: Contains lifestyle/occasion photography + headline textbox. The human/social/food imagery.
- **CLOSE section**: Contains call-to-action text, pricing, or promotional messaging.

### Aspect Ratio & Proportion Rules

- Standard supported ratios: 1:1, 2:1, 1:2, 3:1, 1:3
- SEE section width: logo block width = "a" (shorter edge unit); product area = 1.3a-1.5a wide
- ENGAGE section: text occupies roughly 1/2a height within the engage area
- When material exceeds 1:3 vertical ratio: logo MUST switch to vertical 4:3 orientation
- When height changes, "See" section remains fixed; "Engage" section proportion reduces
- Destination zone: when height changes, "See" and "Engage" stay fixed; "Close" reduces

### Product Presentation Standards

- Products should be shown at slight angle for dimensionality (not flat/head-on)
- Can labels must face forward with brand name clearly legible
- Glass bottle Coca-Cola contour shape must be accurate and recognizable
- Products must look photorealistic (not illustrated/cartoonish)
- Liquid in bottles/glasses should appear natural with appropriate condensation/reflections
- Food items (when present in Engage section) must look appetizing and fresh
